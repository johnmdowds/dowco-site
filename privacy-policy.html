Privacy Policy for ViceVersa

Effective Date: [April 2025]

ViceVersa is committed to protecting your privacy. This app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

Data Storage

All data you enter into ViceVersa is stored locally on your device in a private SQLite database. No personal data is transmitted to external servers.

Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, a device token is generated and stored by Firebase (a service provided by Google) to deliver notifications. This token is not linked to any personal information and is used solely for the purpose of app notifications.

Children

This app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit any data from children under 13 years of age.

Third-Party Services

ViceVersa may use third-party services (such as Firebase) which may collect non-identifiable device data according to their respective privacy policies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@dowco.uk.
